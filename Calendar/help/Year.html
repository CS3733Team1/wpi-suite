Summary

In Year view you can view your schedule in a variety of views or formats, including day, week, month, year, task list, project planner and multi-events. For example, the year view lets you view a month, week and day schedule; the Multi- events lets you view, compare, and manage the schedules of multiple events that have been scheduled.

Navigating Through the Year Calendar View.

The current default page of the calendar is the Year view. It has a lot of functionality button a user can use to navigate through the year. The year view has forward, backward and today button located at the top left corner of the calendar.

By clicking the arrows to the left and right of the view label switches the year back and forth. Today button take the user back to the current year, month and the date whenever the the button is pressed.

In the year view commitments and events that has been scheduled for certain dates are highlighted with a blue color.

To jump to a specific day from the year view, double click on that date and it will open the day in the day view tab.


Current day of the year is highlighted with a yellow color.
