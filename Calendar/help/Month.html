Summary

In Month view you can view your schedule in a variety of views or formats, including day, week, month, year, task list, project planner and multi-events. For example, the month view lets you view a month schedule; the Multi- events lets you view, compare, and manage the schedules of multiple events that have been scheduled.

Navigating Through the Month Calendar View.

Just like the year view, the month view also has a lot of functionality button a user can use to navigate through the month. The month view has forward, backward and today button located at the top left corner of the calendar.

By clicking the arrows to the left and right of the view label switches the month back and forth. Today button display the the current date and day of the month.

In the month view, commitments and events that has been scheduled for certain dates are highlighted with color the user pick.

To jump to a specific day from the month view, double click on that date and it will open the day in the day view tab.


Current day of the month is highlighted with a yellow color.
