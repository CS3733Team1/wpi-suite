Summary

In Week view you can view your schedule in a variety of views or formats, including day, week, month, time, task list, project planner and multi-events. For example, the month view lets you view a month schedule; the Multi- events lets you view, compare, and manage the schedules of multiple events that have been scheduled; the time lets you view what time your event and commitments are set to.

Navigating Through the Week Calendar View.

Just like the year and the month view, the week view has a lot of functionality button a user can use to navigate through the week. The week view has forward, backward and today button located at the top left corner of the calendar.

By clicking the arrows to the left and right of the view label switches the week back and forth. Today button display the the current day and date of the week.

In the week view commitments and events that has been scheduled for certain dates are highlighted with a color the user pick.

To jump to a specific day from the week view, double click on the day and it will open the day in the day view tab.


Current day and date of the week is highlighted with a blue color.
