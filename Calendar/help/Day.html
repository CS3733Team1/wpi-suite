Summary

In Day view you can view your schedule in a variety of views or formats, including day, month, time, year, task list, project planner and multi-events. For example, the day view lets you view a day schedule; the Multi- events lets you view, compare, and manage the schedules of multiple events that have been scheduled for the day; the time lets you view what time your event and commitments are set to.

Navigating Through the Day Calendar View.

Just like the year and the month view, the day view has a lot of functionality button a user can use to navigate through the day. The day view has forward, backward and today button located at the top left corner of the calendar.

By clicking the arrows to the left and right of the view label switches the day back and forth. Today button display the the current date and day and time of the day.

In the day view commitments and events that has been scheduled for certain time of the day are highlighted with color the user pick.


The blue bar that stretches under the day represent the current.
